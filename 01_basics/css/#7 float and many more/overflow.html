Overflow:

here the elements inside the div are overfowing its container. so to end this error we use "Overflow techniqes"

  • like auto: computer automatically adjusts the container
  • hidden: keep the content in the container untill its full capacity, after that the content is hidden.
  • scroll: make a scroller to scroll down the content within the container.
  • visible: dosen't matter the size of container, it will spill out the content.
